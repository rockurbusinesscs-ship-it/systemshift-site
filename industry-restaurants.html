What We Build for Restaurants & Hospitality

Automation Systems Built for the Pace of Hospitality

Restaurant operations move fast. Your systems need to move faster. We build automation that handles the operational details so your team can focus on the guest experience.

📅

Reservation Confirmation Systems

No-shows are not just an inconvenience -- they are a direct hit to your revenue. Every empty table during peak hours is money you cannot recover. We build multi-touch reservation confirmation sequences that dramatically reduce no-shows. When a guest books, they receive an immediate confirmation via text and email. Twenty-four hours before, they get a reminder with easy options to confirm, cancel, or reschedule. Two hours before, a final confirmation text goes out. Guests who do not respond get a courtesy call from your host team, prompted by the system. For large parties and private events, we add deposit collection at the time of booking so commitment is built in from the start. Most restaurants see no-show rates drop from 15-20% down to under 5%.

👥

Staff Scheduling Automation

Scheduling staff is one of the most time-consuming management tasks in any restaurant. You need the right number of servers, cooks, and support staff based on reservation volume, day of the week, weather, local events, and historical traffic patterns. We build scheduling systems that factor in all of these variables and generate optimized schedules automatically. Staff receive their schedules via text and can request swaps directly in the system. Managers approve with a single tap. When someone calls out last-minute, the system automatically notifies available staff based on their roles and preferences. No more group texts, no more frantic phone calls, no more managers spending three hours a week building schedules by hand.

⭐

Review Response Automation

Reviews make or break restaurants. A single unanswered negative review on Google or Yelp can steer dozens of potential guests to your competitors. But responding to every review takes time you do not have during a dinner rush. We build review monitoring and response systems that track new reviews across Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, and Facebook in real time. Positive reviews trigger a thank-you response from a library of templates that rotate to keep responses fresh. Negative reviews alert you immediately with a suggested response that acknowledges the issue and invites the guest to connect privately. We also build post-visit feedback sequences that catch unhappy guests before they post publicly, giving you a chance to make things right.

📝

Waitlist Management

When your restaurant is full on a Friday night, how you handle the wait determines whether guests stick around or walk down the street. We build digital waitlist systems that replace the clipboard and the shouting. Guests join via text message, your website, or a tablet at the host stand. They receive real-time wait time updates on their phone, so they can grab a drink at the bar or browse nearby shops instead of hovering by the host stand. When their table is ready, an automatic notification goes out. Guests who do not respond within a configurable window are removed from the list with a polite message. Your host team manages the waitlist from a simple dashboard and can see capacity, average wait times, and table turn rates at a glance.

🍴

Catering & Events Pipeline

Catering inquiries and private event bookings are high-value opportunities that often fall through the cracks because the process is too manual. Someone fills out a form, you email them back two days later, they have already booked with someone else. We build complete catering pipelines with automated inquiry responses, follow-up sequences, proposal generation, contract and deposit collection, event timeline management, kitchen prep checklists, and post-event follow-ups. Every inquiry gets a response within minutes. The pipeline tracks each event from first inquiry to final payment. Nothing gets lost, no details get missed, and your catering revenue grows because you are closing faster than your competitors.

📈

Guest Loyalty & Re-Engagement

The most profitable guests are the ones who come back regularly. We build loyalty and re-engagement systems that track guest visit frequency, preferences, and spending. Guests who have not visited in 30 days get a re-engagement message with a reason to return -- a new menu item, a seasonal special, or a simple "we miss you" note. Birthday and anniversary automation sends personalized offers at the right time. High-value regulars get VIP treatment with early access to events, special menu previews, and personalized recognition that makes them feel valued. All of this runs automatically based on your POS data and guest profiles.

Problems We Solve

The Five Operations Problems Eating Into Your Restaurant's Profits

Every restaurant deals with these issues. The ones that automate them gain a measurable competitive advantage.

Pain Point

No-Show Reservations That Waste Your Best Tables

Friday night, your restaurant is fully booked. You turned away walk-ins because every table was reserved. Then three tables no-show. Those covers are gone forever. You cannot recoup that revenue. Your staff is standing around during what should have been your busiest service. This happens every weekend and it compounds over time -- hundreds of lost covers per month, thousands of dollars in revenue that evaporates because guests did not show up and you had no system to prevent it.

The SystemShift Solution

Our multi-touch confirmation system engages guests at three strategic intervals before their reservation. The immediate confirmation anchors the commitment. The 24-hour reminder makes canceling easy so your table can be released and refilled. The 2-hour final check catches last-minute changes. For large parties, deposits are collected at booking. Restaurants using our system see no-show rates drop below 5%, which on a 100-cover restaurant translates to recovering 10-15 covers per week that would have been lost.

Pain Point

Staff Scheduling That Consumes Management Time

Every week, your manager sits down and tries to puzzle together a schedule that accounts for availability, seniority, labor costs, anticipated traffic, and the fact that three people requested the same night off. The schedule goes out, immediately triggers complaints, and inevitably someone calls out on the busiest night. The manager then spends an hour texting through the roster trying to find a replacement while also trying to run the restaurant. This cycle repeats endlessly.

The SystemShift Solution

Our scheduling system ingests reservation data, historical traffic patterns, and staff availability to generate optimized schedules automatically. Staff receive schedules via text and can swap shifts through the system with manager approval. Callout coverage requests go out automatically to available, qualified staff based on their roles and response history. Managers go from spending 3+ hours per week on scheduling to spending 15 minutes reviewing and approving an auto-generated schedule.

Pain Point

Review Management That Falls Through the Cracks

A guest posts a one-star review about a bad experience three weeks ago. You find it two weeks later while scrolling Google. By then, dozens of potential guests have seen it sitting there unanswered, which looks like you do not care. Meanwhile, your regulars who had great experiences never leave reviews because nobody asked them. Your online reputation is shaped by the loudest complainers while your biggest fans stay silent.

The SystemShift Solution

Our review automation monitors Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, and Facebook in real time. New reviews trigger instant alerts. Positive reviews get automatic thank-you responses from a rotating template library. Negative reviews alert your management team immediately with a suggested response. Meanwhile, post-visit feedback requests go out to recent guests. Happy guests are guided to leave public reviews. Unhappy guests are routed to private feedback first. Your review volume increases, your rating improves, and nothing sits unanswered.

Pain Point

Slow Table Turns During Peak Hours

Your dining room is full but tables are turning slowly. Guests finished 20 minutes ago but are lingering because the check took too long to process. Meanwhile, your waitlist is growing and guests are starting to leave. The host cannot accurately estimate wait times because they have no visibility into which tables are about to turn. Every 10 minutes of unnecessary table occupation during peak hours is lost revenue.

The SystemShift Solution

We build table management systems that give your host team real-time visibility into table status, course progression, and estimated turn times. Automated alerts notify servers when tables have been in post-payment mode for too long. Digital check presentation speeds up the payment process. The waitlist system syncs with table status so wait time estimates are accurate. Guests get notified the moment their table is ready, minimizing the gap between turnover and seating. Faster turns mean more covers per night without adding a single table.

Pain Point

Catering Inquiries That Go Unanswered

Catering and private events are high-margin revenue streams for restaurants, but the inquiry-to-booking process is almost always manual and slow. Someone fills out a form on your website. The email lands in a manager's inbox between 47 other messages. Three days later, they draft a response. By then, the prospect has already booked with the restaurant that responded in an hour. You are losing five-figure catering contracts because your response time cannot keep up with demand.

The SystemShift Solution

Our catering pipeline captures inquiries and responds within minutes with a personalized acknowledgment that includes your catering menu, sample pricing, and a booking link for a consultation call. Automated follow-ups nurture the lead through proposal review, contract signing, and deposit collection. The event timeline is managed in a pipeline with kitchen prep reminders, setup checklists, and day-of coordination. Post-event, an automated follow-up thanks the host and requests a review. Your catering close rate improves because you are always the fastest to respond.
