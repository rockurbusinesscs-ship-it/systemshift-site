What We Build for Insurance Agencies

Automation Systems That Protect and Grow Your Book of Business

Insurance agencies run on relationships and data. Our automation systems ensure no relationship is neglected and no data point is missed -- whether you write personal lines, commercial, or both.

📅

Renewal Reminder Sequences

Policy renewals are the lifeblood of an insurance agency. Every renewal that lapses is revenue that took years to build, gone in an instant. We build multi-touch renewal sequences that begin 90 days before expiration and escalate through the renewal window. The first touchpoint is an educational message about reviewing coverage. At 60 days, the system sends a policy review appointment link. At 30 days, a coverage comparison showing what has changed. At 14 days, urgency messaging about coverage gaps. Your producers are notified at each stage so they can add personal outreach where needed. For high-value policies, the system triggers a personal call assignment. No renewal gets forgotten, no matter how large your book grows.

📈

Quote Follow-Up Funnels

You invest time and effort in generating quotes, but the follow-up process determines whether those quotes convert to bound policies. Most agencies quote and then wait for the prospect to call back. We build structured quote follow-up funnels: immediate confirmation that the quote is being prepared, delivery with a clear comparison summary, a follow-up asking if they have questions, a second touchpoint with a testimonial from a similar client, and a final message before the quote expires. Each message is personalized to the coverage type and the prospect's stated priorities. This systematic approach typically doubles the quote-to-bind conversion rate because no quote dies from lack of follow-up.

🔍

Cross-Sell Identification & Triggers

Your existing policyholders are your best prospects for additional lines of coverage. The auto-only client who does not have homeowners. The business owner with a BOP but no workers comp. The family with auto and home but no umbrella. We build cross-sell identification systems that analyze your book of business, score each client for cross-sell potential based on their existing coverage gaps, and trigger personalized outreach at optimal moments. Those moments include renewal time, after a life event, when they engage with educational content, or when they contact your agency for service. Each outreach is educational first, showing the client why the coverage matters, not just pushing a product.

📄

Document Collection Automation

Insurance applications and claims require extensive documentation. Driver's licenses, property photos, financial statements, loss runs, certificates -- the list varies by line and carrier. Chasing this paperwork manually is one of the most time-consuming tasks in any agency. We build automated document collection workflows that request specific documents via email and text, provide secure upload links, track which documents have been received and which are outstanding, send automatic reminders for missing items, and notify your team when the package is complete. For commercial accounts, the system manages multi-document requests across multiple contacts within the same account. Your team stops chasing paperwork and starts closing business.

🚀

Carrier Submission Workflows

Preparing and submitting applications to carriers is a multi-step process that involves gathering client data, formatting it for each carrier's requirements, submitting through portals or email, and tracking the status of each submission. We build workflows that organize collected client data into submission-ready packages, route applications to appropriate carriers based on risk appetite and client profile, track submission status with automated follow-ups to carriers on pending decisions, and notify your team when quotes are returned. While we cannot log into carrier portals directly, we automate everything around the submission process, reducing prep time per submission from 30-45 minutes to under 10.

🤖

AI-Powered Client Communication

Your clients have questions that do not require a licensed producer to answer: When is my next payment due? Can I get a copy of my ID card? What is my deductible? What does my policy cover? We build AI chat and voice agents trained on your agency's policies, procedures, and FAQs that handle these routine inquiries 24/7. ID card requests are fulfilled automatically. Payment questions are answered from billing data. Coverage questions are answered accurately with appropriate disclaimers. Complex questions and anything requiring licensed advice are escalated to your team with full context. Your producers spend less time on service calls and more time on revenue-generating activities.

Problems We Solve

The Five Revenue Threats in Every Insurance Agency

These problems cost agencies policies, revenue, and growth every month. Each one has a proven automation solution.

Pain Point

Policy Renewals That Slip Through the Cracks

Your agency writes hundreds or thousands of policies. Each one has a different renewal date. Your AMS flags upcoming renewals, but the follow-up is manual. A CSR sends a letter, maybe an email. If the client does not respond, the renewal might get a second touch -- or it might not. During busy season, renewals get pushed to the back burner. Every lapsed policy is not just lost commission -- it is a client who will now shop the market and may never come back. The cost of replacing that revenue far exceeds the cost of retaining it.

The SystemShift Solution

Our renewal automation begins 90 days out with a coverage review invitation and escalates through multiple touchpoints at 60, 30, 14, and 7 days. Each message is personalized to the policy type and the client's history with your agency. Producers are notified when high-value renewals need personal attention. The system tracks response and engagement, so you know which clients are at risk before their policy lapses. Agencies using our system see renewal rates improve by 10-15%, which on a $2M book translates to $200K-$300K in protected annual revenue.

Pain Point

Quote Follow-Up That Dies After the First Email

A prospect requests a quote. Your producer puts together a competitive option and sends it over. Then silence. The prospect is shopping multiple agencies and the one who follows up most effectively wins. But your producer has 15 other quotes to prepare today and follow-up falls to the bottom of the list. By the time they circle back, the prospect has already bound with a competitor. You invested 30-60 minutes in underwriting and quoting that prospect, and you lost them because of a $0 follow-up email that never got sent.

The SystemShift Solution

Our quote follow-up funnel fires automatically the moment a quote is delivered. The prospect receives a summary email highlighting the coverage strengths of your recommendation. Two days later, a follow-up with a testimonial from a similar client. Five days later, a message addressing common objections for that coverage type. The final touchpoint before quote expiration creates urgency. Every message includes a one-click link to schedule a call with the producer. This systematic approach typically doubles quote-to-bind rates because follow-up is consistent, timely, and impossible to forget.

Pain Point

Cross-Sell Opportunities Hiding in Your Book

You have 2,000 personal lines clients. How many of them have only one policy with your agency? 40%? 60%? Each one is a cross-sell opportunity that someone on your team should be working. But nobody has time to analyze the book, identify the gaps, and make the outreach. So those single-line clients stay single-line -- until a competitor bundles them away with a better deal on the package. You are losing clients not because of price, but because you never offered them the convenience of consolidation.

The SystemShift Solution

We build cross-sell engines that analyze your book of business and identify every client with coverage gaps. Each opportunity is scored by value and likelihood. The system triggers personalized outreach at the optimal moments: during renewal, after a life event trigger, when they call for service, or when they engage with educational content. The outreach is educational -- showing the client why umbrella coverage matters, why bundling saves money, why their business needs cyber liability. Your producers receive hot cross-sell leads with context, not cold call lists.

Pain Point

Document Collection That Buries Your CSRs

A commercial account needs a driver's license for every listed driver, three years of loss runs, a current ACORD application, property photos, and financial statements. Your CSR sends the request. The client sends two of the five items. Your CSR follows up. The client sends one more. Another follow-up. Two weeks later, you are still missing the loss runs and the submission deadline is tomorrow. This back-and-forth is happening on 10-15 accounts simultaneously, and your CSR is spending half their day chasing paperwork instead of processing policies.

The SystemShift Solution

Our document collection system sends a single request with secure upload links for each required document. The system tracks which documents have been received and which are outstanding. Automatic reminders go out for missing items at configurable intervals. The client gets a simple dashboard showing their completion status. Your team gets notified when the package is complete and ready for submission. For multi-contact commercial accounts, the system routes requests to the right contacts at the insured. Collection time drops from weeks of back-and-forth to days of automated follow-up.

Pain Point

Carrier Submissions That Consume Producer Time

Preparing a submission for a commercial account takes 30-45 minutes per carrier. Gathering the data, formatting it for that carrier's requirements, compiling the supplemental documents, and sending it through the right channel. If you are submitting to four carriers, that is two to three hours per account. Your producers should be selling, but instead they are doing data entry and document formatting. The administrative burden of the submission process is one of the biggest productivity drains in any insurance agency.

The SystemShift Solution

We build submission preparation workflows that compile client data from your AMS and document collection system into organized submission packages. Carrier routing logic directs each submission to the appropriate carriers based on risk characteristics and appetite. Status tracking monitors each submission through the underwriting process. Automated follow-ups nudge carriers on pending decisions. Your producers go from spending hours on submission prep to spending minutes reviewing and approving an auto-assembled package.
