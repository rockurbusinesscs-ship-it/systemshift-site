What We'll Cover in Your Audit

We look at six core areas of your business to find where time and money are leaking.

01

Lead Generation

How leads find you and what happens when they do. Are you capturing every opportunity or letting them slip?

02

Sales Pipeline

How deals move from inquiry to close. Where are prospects dropping off and why?

03

Marketing Systems

Campaign automation, tracking, and attribution. Are you measuring what matters?

04

Operations

SOPs, task management, and team workflows. How much time is lost to manual processes?

05

Client Experience

Onboarding, communication, and retention. What does the journey look like after they sign?

06

Tech Stack

Tool overlap, integration gaps, and monthly costs. Are you paying for software you're not using?
