What We Build for Financial Professionals

Financial Services Automation That Grows AUM

Every system is designed for the unique demands of financial services -- compliance, confidentiality, and the high-touch client relationships that drive AUM growth. No generic CRM setups. Just purpose-built automation for advisors, planners, and wealth managers.

📧

Compliant Drip Sequences

Marketing automation built with SEC and FINRA compliance in mind from the ground up. Every email template includes appropriate disclosures. Every message is logged and archived for your compliance records. Your compliance officer approves templates before they enter the system, and the AI never deviates from approved copy. We build educational nurture sequences -- retirement planning tips, market commentary frameworks, tax-planning reminders -- that position you as the expert without crossing compliance lines. Prospects receive consistent, valuable touchpoints for months while you focus on serving existing clients. Built on GoHighLevel with compliance-grade recordkeeping through Zapier integrations to your archival systems. Each sequence is tagged, timestamped, and searchable for audits.

📋

Digital Client Onboarding

The gap between "yes, I want to work with you" and "everything is signed and funded" is where financial advisors lose momentum -- and sometimes lose clients entirely. Our digital onboarding system cuts that timeline by 60-70%. When a prospect commits, the system immediately sends a welcome sequence with digital forms for account opening paperwork, KYC documentation, risk tolerance questionnaires, IPS agreements, and beneficiary designations. Everything is mobile-friendly. Clients can upload documents from their phone, e-sign agreements, and complete questionnaires in their own time. The system tracks exactly where each client is in the onboarding process and sends automatic reminders for incomplete steps. You never have to chase paperwork again. Your team gets alerts when documents need review, and the client gets confirmation when each step is complete. The result: clients feel taken care of from day one, and your team spends 70% less time on administrative onboarding tasks.

📅

Portfolio Review Reminders

Regular portfolio reviews are the cornerstone of client retention -- and the first thing that slips when advisors get busy. Our system tracks each client's last review date and automatically sends personalized reminders when their next review is due. Quarterly reviewers get quarterly reminders. Annual reviewers get annual check-ins. The system also watches for life-event triggers that you log -- marriage, new child, job change, inheritance, retirement date approaching -- and prompts you to schedule an off-cycle review when something changes. Each reminder includes a one-click booking link so clients can choose their own time. No phone tag. No back-and-forth emails. Review completion rates typically jump 35-50% because the friction of scheduling disappears. And every completed review is an opportunity to discuss additional planning needs, referrals, and consolidating outside assets.

📞

Appointment Automation

Prospect wants a meeting. They call your office. Nobody picks up because your team is in client meetings. They leave a voicemail. Your assistant calls back 4 hours later. Prospect already booked with another advisor. Sound familiar? Our appointment automation eliminates this entirely. Prospects book directly from your website, email signatures, social media profiles, or text messages. The system syncs with your calendar in real time, respects your meeting preferences (no back-to-back discovery calls, buffer time between meetings, no meetings during your planning blocks), and sends confirmation texts and emails immediately. Reminder sequences go out 24 hours and 1 hour before each meeting. If someone needs to reschedule, they do it themselves through the system -- no phone tag required. No-show rates typically drop 30-40% with automated reminders alone. For prospects, the system also sends a pre-meeting questionnaire so you walk into every discovery call already knowing their financial situation, goals, and concerns.

📈

AUM & Pipeline Tracking

Your CRM should tell you more than just contact information. Our pipeline system tracks every prospect from first touchpoint to onboarded client, with projected AUM at each stage. You can see at a glance: how many prospects are in your pipeline, what their combined potential AUM is, which ones have gone cold, and which ones need a follow-up today. For existing clients, the system tracks AUM milestones, fee breakpoints, and consolidation opportunities. When a client's assets approach a fee breakpoint or when you notice outside accounts during a review, the system flags it for follow-up. We also build referral tracking so you know exactly which clients are sending you business and can prioritize those relationships accordingly. All of this data feeds into a dashboard that gives you a real-time picture of your practice's health -- not just revenue today, but projected growth based on your pipeline and retention rates.

⭐

Review & Referral Campaigns

The best financial advisor marketing is a satisfied client telling their friends. But most advisors rely on passive referrals -- hoping clients will mention them when the topic comes up. Our referral system makes it active and systematic. After every successful portfolio review or financial plan delivery, the system sends a personalized thank-you message with a simple ask: "If you know anyone who could benefit from the same kind of planning, I would love the introduction." These messages are timed, personalized, and non-pushy. The system also manages your Google and LinkedIn review presence. Happy clients get a one-tap review request after positive interactions. The result is a steady stream of social proof that builds trust with prospects who are researching advisors online. Financial advisors using systematic referral campaigns see 25-40% more referrals compared to advisors who rely on organic word-of-mouth alone.

Common Challenges We Solve

Financial Services Pain Points and How We Fix Them

Every one of these problems costs you AUM, client satisfaction, or both. Every one is solvable with the right automation.

The Problem

Compliance Paperwork Bottleneck

Every new client means a stack of forms -- account applications, KYC documents, risk tolerance questionnaires, IPS agreements, beneficiary designations. Your team spends hours printing, mailing, chasing signatures, and filing. Meanwhile, the excited new client's enthusiasm cools while they wait for "the paperwork phase" to end.

The SystemShift Fix

Digital onboarding with e-signatures, automated document collection, and progress tracking. Clients complete everything from their phone. The system reminds them about incomplete steps. Your team gets notified when documents are ready for review. Onboarding that used to take 2-3 weeks now takes 3-5 days.

The Problem

Client Onboarding Friction

The gap between a prospect saying "yes" and becoming a fully onboarded client is the most dangerous period in the advisory relationship. Every day of delay is a chance for buyer's remorse, a competitor's call, or simple inertia to kill the deal. Yet most firms make onboarding harder than it needs to be with clunky paper processes and unclear next steps.

The SystemShift Fix

A seamless digital onboarding flow that guides the client step-by-step: welcome message, digital forms, document uploads, e-signatures, and confirmation -- all automated, all mobile-friendly. The client always knows what to do next, and your team always knows where each client stands in the process.

The Problem

Missed Portfolio Reviews

When you are busy with new prospects and market volatility, existing client reviews slip. Clients who do not hear from you start wondering if you are paying attention to their money. Some quietly transfer to another advisor. You never even knew there was a problem until the ACAT notice arrives.

The SystemShift Fix

Automated review scheduling that tracks every client's review cycle and sends timely, personalized reminders with one-click booking. Life-event triggers prompt off-cycle reviews. You stay proactive instead of reactive, and clients feel like they are your only client -- even if you manage hundreds of relationships.

The Problem

Appointment No-Shows

You blocked 90 minutes for a discovery meeting. You prepared a preliminary plan. You pulled their Social Security statement and ran projections. They do not show up. No call, no text, no email. You wasted your most valuable resource -- your time -- and now you have to reschedule and rebuild that momentum from scratch.

The SystemShift Fix

Multi-channel appointment reminders (text + email) at 24 hours and 1 hour before the meeting. Pre-meeting questionnaires that create investment in the conversation before it happens. Easy rescheduling that keeps the relationship alive. No-show rates drop 30-40% with these simple automations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Financial Services Automation FAQ

Is automated marketing compliant for financial advisors?
Yes, when built correctly. Our drip campaigns and automated communications are designed with SEC, FINRA, and state insurance compliance in mind. Every automated message can include required disclosures, is logged for recordkeeping, and goes through an approval workflow before going live. We build the guardrails in -- your compliance officer reviews templates before they enter the system. All messages are archived and searchable for audits.
Can SystemShift integrate with financial planning software like Redtail or Wealthbox?
Yes. We build on GoHighLevel and connect it to your existing tools through Zapier and direct API integrations. Common integrations include Redtail CRM, Wealthbox, RightCapital, MoneyGuidePro, Riskalyze, and custodian platforms like Schwab and Fidelity. We can sync client data, trigger workflows from planning milestones, and keep everything in one dashboard without replacing the tools your team already knows.
How does automated onboarding work for financial advisory clients?
When a prospect becomes a client, the system automatically sends a welcome sequence with digital forms for account opening, KYC documentation, risk tolerance questionnaires, and beneficiary designations. Each step is tracked so you can see exactly where each client is in the onboarding process. Reminders go out automatically for incomplete steps, and your team gets alerts when documents need review. The entire process is mobile-friendly -- clients can complete everything from their phone.
How much does financial advisor automation cost?

A basic appointment and follow-up system might be $500-$1,500, while a full advisory practice automation suite with compliant drip campaigns, digital onboarding, and portfolio review scheduling runs $3,000-$5,000+. Monthly retainers start at $500/month. Every project includes a free practice audit, 30-day testing period, and training for your team.
Can the AI handle client communications for a financial advisory firm?
The AI handles scheduling, appointment reminders, initial inquiry responses, and administrative communications. It does not provide financial advice or make investment recommendations. All client-facing messages are pre-approved templates with appropriate disclosures. The AI qualifies prospects and books consultations -- the advice comes from you. This keeps you compliant while dramatically improving response times and client experience.
How do portfolio review reminders work?
The system tracks each client's last review date and automatically sends personalized reminders when their next review is due -- quarterly, semi-annually, or annually based on your schedule. Clients can book their review meeting directly from the reminder email or text. The system also triggers reviews based on life events if you log them -- marriage, new child, job change, retirement approaching -- so you are always proactive, never reactive. Review completion rates typically increase 35-50%.
