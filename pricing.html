How It Works

Every Project Starts With a Free Audit

Every project starts with a free Systems Audit where we map your bottlenecks and design the solution. After that, you get a detailed proposal with exact costs before any work begins.

Our pricing depends on three things:

  1. 1 Complexity of your workflows
  2. 2 Number of integrations needed
  3. 3 Level of AI involved

No hourly billing. No scope creep surprises. Just a clear project price with milestones. Whether you're a service business in Greensboro, a startup in Winston-Salem, or scaling across the Triangle -- you'll know exactly what you're paying before we write a single line of code.
