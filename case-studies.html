Legal Tech Email Automation

Cold Email Pipeline Built From Scratch in 3 Weeks

ESQgo - Amazon Seller Suspension Recovery

ESQgo helps suspended Amazon sellers get reinstated, but had no outbound system to reach potential clients. Their team of three was doing everything manually - finding leads, writing individual emails, and tracking conversations in their heads. They needed to reach thousands of suspended sellers with personalized outreach at scale.

What We Built

Complete cold email automation pipeline: 5-email outreach sequence with A/B test variants, Airtable automation scripts for variable injection (seller name, revenue figures, suspension type), currency formatting, and stage automation. Set up dedicated email domains with full DNS configuration and 2-week warm-up protocol to protect deliverability. End-to-end QA testing before go-live.
